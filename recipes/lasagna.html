Lasagna Recipe

Description

A well plated lasagna

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Sauce
  • Beef

Steps

  1. Step 1: Sautee the beef in a pan
  2. Step 2: Boil the pasta until al dente
  3. Step 3: Lay one layer of the pasta at the bottom of the pan and spreach one layer of beef and repeat until all Ingredients are used up
  4. Step 4: Bake at 350F for 30 minutes
